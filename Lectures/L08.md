**1. Defining Networks and Social Networks**
*   **Network:** Functions as both a noun (an interconnected group of people or things) and a verb (interacting with others to gain connections or personal advancement). 
*   **Social Network:** Websites that allow people to create online connections. They grow organically as individuals invite others to join their personal networks, who in turn invite more people.

**2. Digital News Consumption and Trust (2026 Context)**
*   **Top Platforms for News:** In Malaysia, the top platforms used for news are WhatsApp (50%), Facebook (49%), and TikTok (45%). 
*   **Top News Sources:** Malaysiakini and Astro Awani have the highest weekly online reach, while TV3 dominates offline TV reach. 
*   **Public Trust:** Overall trust in news is relatively low at 30%, and a significant majority of people (63%) are highly concerned about online misinformation.

**3. Network Effects and Metcalfe's Law**
*   **Core Concept:** A social network relies heavily on "network effects," meaning it only attracts users if their friends are also on the site. 
*   **Metcalfe’s Law:** The value of a network increases proportionally to the **square of the number of its users** ($Value = n^2$). 

**4. Social Bookmarking and Folksonomy**
*   **Folksonomy:** A combination of the words "Folks" and "Taxonomy." It refers to users sharing, categorizing, and organizing internet bookmarks (e.g., using platforms like Reddit and Pinterest).
*   **Advantages:** It allows users to access bookmarks from any computer, discover new sites via searchable tags, and find content based on community recommendations and user reviews. 

**5. The Impact of Social Media**
*   **Double-Edged Sword:** Social media has both positive and negative impacts on society. 
*   **Negative Consequences:** The slides specifically highlight the dangers of rapid spreading of unverified news (jokingly compared to the speed of light by a meme), the reckless sharing of victims' private information during accidents, and the manipulation of social media to actively undermine global democracy.
